# NexuzyAI — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

---

## 🔒 We Do Not Collect Any Data

NexuzyAI is built on a simple principle: **your conversations are yours alone.**
All AI inference runs entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

---

## 1. No Data Collection

We do **not** collect, store, transmit, or share:
- Conversation history or messages
- Voice recordings or transcriptions
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage statistics or analytics
- Any other personal information

---

## 2. On-Device AI Processing

NexuzyAI uses the **Qwen3** language model compiled via [MLC-LLM](https://github.com/mlc-ai/mlc-llm).
All inference runs **100% locally** using your phone's CPU/GPU.
Your messages never leave your device.

---

## 3. Location

Location access (GPS) is requested **only** to provide contextual answers
(e.g. local weather, nearby info). Location data is processed locally
and is never transmitted to any server.

---

## 4. Microphone

Microphone access is used only for **voice input** via Android's built-in
`SpeechRecognizer`. We do not record, store, or transmit audio.

---

## 5. Internet Access

Internet is used only for:
| Purpose | Service | Personal data sent? |
|---|---|---|
| Weather | [Open-Meteo](https://open-meteo.com) | None (only coordinates) |
| News headlines | Google News RSS | None |
| Ads | Google AdMob | Per Google's policy |
| Model download | HuggingFace (first launch) | None |

---

## 6. Advertising

NexuzyAI uses **Google AdMob** for in-app advertising. Ads may use your
device's advertising ID. You can opt out via:
`Android Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID`

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

## 7. Open Source

NexuzyAI is **fully open source**. Every line of code is publicly auditable:

🐙 **https://github.com/david0154/NexuzyAI-Android**

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

Since we collect no data whatsoever, NexuzyAI is safe for all ages.
We do not knowingly interact with data from children under 13.
